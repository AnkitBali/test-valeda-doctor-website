About Valeda Herbal Private Limited

Establishment

The establishment dates back to 1880. 5 generations of establishment is presented as a short summary below. The commercial name was established in 1988. Dr Seema Bali has been using these products since 32 Years in her own clinical practice.

1st Generation - 1880 – Arjan Dev Bali

The Foundation of Valeda dates back in 1880 when keeping up the tradition of Herbal Care and Cure was a noble soul Arjan Dev Bali. He dedicated his life in formulation and dispensing herbal decoctions in township of Jabailpur near Rawalpindi with backdrop of Margalla Hills on the Potwar Plateau in Himalayas.

2nd Generation - 1920 – Gokul Chand Bali

The healing touches of rare Medicinal herbs continued with his talented Son Gokul Chand Bali between the years 1920 to 1950. The place of this Great traditional herbal research changed after partition of India. Still maintaining the Himalayan touch, the venue of activities for incorporating invaluable herbal knowledge to formulation for benefit of mankind was now in foothills near Dehradun.

3rd Generation - 1950 – Pishori Lal Bali

The mantle of this herbal dynasty was with knowledgeable Pishori Lal Bali who continued traditional herbal magic till 1996.

4th Generation – 1986 Mr Rajesh Bali , Mr Manoj Bali

By this time his scientifically educated sons had joined the family tradition and what was continuing as Nature's Service to Mankind shaped in as commercial business activity from 1986 under the brand VALEDA HERBAL.

1986 – Dr Seema Bali

Dr Seema Bali, a renowned Doctor started researching and formulating unique herbal formulations for her patients in her personal OPD.
The two generations came together, where Dr Seema Bali for the 1st time added Medical and Clinical research based Formulations of her personal OPD to 4 Generation Formulations of Mr Rajesh Bali and Mr Manoj Bali.

1990 – Mrs Alka Bhalla (B.Pharma) ( Now working in leading a reputed pharma company QC department in Toronto ) and Dr Anu Puri, Ph.D (Scientist, National Cancer Institute, NIH, USA) served in 1990's as the advisor for Valeda Products formulations, composition and ingredients. Dr Anu Puri is an expert in the area of lipids, biomembranes, viruses, HIV/AIDS research, nanotechnology and drug delivery. She also collaborates with BITS, Pilani and HAU, Hisar and served as the advisor for Uttrakhand Biotechnology council.

5th Generation – 2016 - Dr Ankita Bali

Dr Ankita Bali joined her family business in 2016. Currently, with the team of young, energetic doctors in Valeda, she is working to take her legacy to next level.
